About Us

Welcome to Magallanes Landscaping, a family-run landscaping company with a rich history dating back 20 years. Founded by a father-son duo who shared a love for the natural beauty of Western Washington, our company has grown into a symbol of commitment to enhancing our environment, one garden at a time.

Our mission is to harmonize the beauty of nature with the comforts of home. We believe in creating stunning landscapes that not only enhance the aesthetic appeal of your outdoor spaces but also serve as a sanctuary for you and local wildlife.

What sets us apart in the landscaping industry is our unique blend of artistry, expertise, and deep respect for nature. One of our notable projects includes the Cayson Fields Development, where we transformed an open field into a vibrant green space, teeming with native plants and attracting local wildlife.

We are proud advocates of sustainable landscaping practices. We focus on incorporating native plants that thrive in the local climate, promoting biodiversity, and reducing water usage. Our projects are designed to be not just beautiful, but eco-friendly and low maintenance.

At Magallanes Landscaping, customer satisfaction is not just a promise but an ethos we live by. From the initial consultation to the final implementation, we collaborate closely with our clients, ensuring their visions are brought to life down to the last detail.

We uphold the values of integrity, sustainability, and craftsmanship. These guide us in every project we undertake, enabling us to deliver landscaping solutions that leave a lasting impact. Come, join us in our journey to make Western Washington greener, one garden at a time.
